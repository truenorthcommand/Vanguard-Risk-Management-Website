About Vanguard

Founded by veterans of government close protection and specialist military units, Vanguard Risk Management brings an intelligence-led approach to every engagement. Headquartered in London, we operate globally with discretion, precision, and an unwavering commitment to client safety.

Our Ethos

We believe effective security is invisible. Our operatives integrate seamlessly into our clients' lives and business environments, providing protection that is felt but never seen. Every decision is guided by intelligence, every action measured against the highest professional standards.

Our Team

Our personnel are drawn from the upper echelons of UK Special Forces, government protection commands, and intelligence services. Every operative holds a valid SIA licence and has passed BS 7858 enhanced vetting. We maintain the standards that defined our previous service.

London Headquarters

Operating from our discreet headquarters in Central London, we maintain a permanent operations room capable of coordinating global deployments. Our location provides immediate access to major transport hubs and the City's corporate ecosystem.

Global Network

Through established partnerships with vetted security professionals across six continents, we deliver consistent service quality regardless of location. Our advance teams prepare the ground before every deployment, ensuring seamless operations in any environment.
