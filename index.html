About Vanguard

Setting New Standards in Security Excellence

Vanguard Risk Management is a London-based specialist security and risk management company delivering discreet, intelligence-led solutions to clients across the globe.

Headquartered in the heart of London, we support a select portfolio of high-profile and ultra-high-net-worth individuals, families, and corporate clients who demand the highest standards of professionalism, discretion, and operational excellence.

Our team is composed of highly experienced security professionals who have dedicated their careers to the private security and risk management sector. With backgrounds spanning close protection, intelligence, maritime security, and crisis management, our personnel bring extensive operational expertise and a culturally aware approach to every assignment.

We maintain a rigorous recruitment and vetting process, selecting only individuals with proven track records, exceptional training, and uncompromising integrity. All operatives are fully licensed by the Security Industry Authority (SIA) in accordance with the Private Security Industry Act 2001 and are trained well beyond the minimum industry requirements.

In addition, all operatives undergo enhanced screening in line with BS 7858, providing our clients with complete confidence and peace of mind.

At Vanguard Risk Management, our mission is to deliver exceptional security solutions with innovation, efficiency, and integrity—setting new industry standards while providing complete protection and reassurance.

Our Approach

Trusted Relationships. Tailored Solutions.

We understand that modern clients expect more than traditional security services. They require trusted professionals who can seamlessly integrate into their personal and professional environments while adding value beyond physical protection alone.
